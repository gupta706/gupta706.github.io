Welcome to the new site. Beyond the fresh, Ohio State–branded look, the real
upgrade is under the hood: the whole site is now generated from a handful of
plain-text files.

- **Publications maintain themselves.** I add a BibTeX entry to a `.bib` file,
  push, and the [Publications]({{ '/publications/' | relative_url }}) page
  rebuilds — grouped by year, split into journal and conference work, with links
  and copy-paste BibTeX.
- **Blog posts are just Markdown.** Like this one.
- **Projects, talks, courses, and students** each live in one editable file.

If you're a student curious about the group's work, the
[Research]({{ '/research/' | relative_url }}) and
[Projects]({{ '/projects/' | relative_url }}) pages are the best place to start.
More notes to come.
